Using your Search Terms Report

Identify negative keywords by reviewing your Search Terms Report. It shows you the actual search terms that have triggered your ad, so you can decide if they're relevant to your business and filter out those that aren't.

Sign in to your AdWords account and follow the steps below.

1. Choose your campaign

  • a Select the Campaigns tab.
  • b Choose your '100 Krafts' campaign.
  • c Click the Keywords tab.

2. View the Search Terms Report

  • a Click the Keyword details drop-down button below the performance graph.
  • b Under SEARCH TERMS, select All. A list of search terms will appear.

3. Identify and add negative keywords

  • a To select the date range of your data, click the date drop-down button in the top right-hand corner of the page.
  • b Identify search terms in the report that are least relevant to your business.
  • c Check the box next to each search term you want to add as a negative keyword.
  • d When you're finished, click Add as negative keyword.
